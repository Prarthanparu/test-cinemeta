ABOUT US

CineMeta combines filmmaking, blockchain, and AI to transform how films are funded, made, distributed, and watched. Its patented Watch-to-Earn model lets viewers solve puzzles in films to unlock real digital asset rewards. The platform integrates traditional film financing with blockchain tokenization, connecting filmmakers directly with audiences and investors. CineMeta's DigitalDVD marketplace offers a revolutionary blockchain-based distribution model where users can own, rent, and trade films as digital assets, creating an alternative to conventional streaming services.
